Why FirstGrid

Most firms sell slide decks. We deliver working analysis, reproducible methods, and engineers who've been in the field.

Code-First Engineering

We don't rely on black-box software or static templates. Our analysis is built on custom tooling and open-source foundations — accurate, transparent, and fully reproducible. Every number we deliver can be audited, reproduced, and challenged.

Deep Domain Expertise

20 years of hands-on distribution system engineering isn't a resume bullet — it's the difference between theoretical answers and practical ones. We've run the studies, seen the failure modes, and know where consulting reports go off the rails.

Open-Source Credibility

We build and maintain open-source tools used by engineers worldwide. Our consultancy isn't a side hustle — it's an extension of the same technical work we do for the open-source community. The code speaks for itself.

Flexible Engagement Model

No retainer minimums, no bloated teams of junior analysts. You work directly with an experienced engineer who understands distribution systems end-to-end. Scale up or down based on what you actually need.

Clear Communication

We translate complex engineering analysis into actionable insights. Your reports won't be buried in jargon — they'll give you the technical depth your stakeholders need and the clarity to make decisions.

Built to Scale

Our tooling and methods are designed for repetition, not one-off projects. As your organization grows and your data matures, our work products scale with you — no re-engineering required.
