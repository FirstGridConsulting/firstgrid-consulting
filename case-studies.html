How a Regional Utility Reduced DER Interconnection Study Time by 60%

The Solution

Capacity Expansion Load Forecasting

Building a 10-Year Distribution Capacity Roadmap for a Growing Municipality

A fast-growing municipality needed to plan distribution infrastructure upgrades to support commercial development and emerging EV charging demand — but didn't have the internal capacity to model multiple growth scenarios.

The Challenge

  • Rapid commercial development in underserved corridors
  • Aging distribution assets with unknown condition data
  • No scenario modeling capability for EV or DER load growth
  • Capital planning deadlines driven by state funding cycles

The Solution

  • Developed multi-scenario load forecasting model (base, high, low)
  • Identified priority feeders requiring immediate upgrade vs. deferrable work
  • Built interactive capacity dashboard for planning team use
  • Delivered state grant-ready technical documentation

Resilience Grid Hardening

Resilience Assessment & Hardening Priorities for a Coastal Utility

A coastal utility needed to prioritize grid hardening investments ahead of hurricane season, with limited capital and competing priorities across a wide geographic footprint.

The Challenge

  • Increasing frequency of severe weather events
  • Limited capital budget for hardening across 2,000+ miles of feeders
  • No data-driven prioritization framework in place
  • Regulatory pressure for improved SAIDI/SAIFI metrics

The Solution

  • Geographic vulnerability assessment across all feeder segments
  • Outage scenario modeling for multiple storm intensities
  • Cost-benefit ranking of hardening measures by segment
  • Microgrid feasibility analysis for critical facilities
