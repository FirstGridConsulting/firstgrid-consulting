Our Services

Comprehensive distribution system engineering — from feasibility studies to long-term strategic planning.

01

DER Integration & Hosting Capacity Studies

As distributed energy resources penetrate deeper into distribution networks, utilities need reliable, repeatable methods to evaluate their impact. We conduct thorough hosting capacity assessments and interconnection studies that give you the data you need to make confident decisions.

What We Deliver

  • Hosting capacity assessments by feeder and circuit
  • Detailed interconnection study reports
  • Power flow and short-circuit analysis
  • Voltage regulation and protection coordination review
  • Recommended mitigation strategies
  • GridEdge-based analysis and custom tooling

Ideal For

  • Utilities evaluating DER interconnection queues
  • Developers planning solar or storage projects
  • Distributors needing rapid capacity screening
  • Regulatory filings requiring technical evidence

  • Utility capital planning inputs

Distribution Capacity Expansion Planning

Long-term grid capacity planning requires more than trend lines. We combine load forecasting, DER penetration scenarios, and asset condition data to build capacity expansion strategies that align with your utility's growth trajectory.

What We Deliver

  • Load forecasting and scenario modeling
  • Grid capacity gap analysis
  • Multi-year expansion roadmap
  • DER impact integration into planning models
  • Priority investment sequencing

Ideal For

  • Utilities planning for 5–10 year horizons
  • Organizations managing rapid load growth
  • Long-range resource planning cycles
  • Regulatory compliance for capacity standards

04

Distribution Resilience Assessment

Extreme weather, aging infrastructure, and cyber threats — the risks to distribution systems are mounting. We help utilities identify vulnerabilities, prioritize hardening measures, and build resilience strategies that protect service continuity.

What We Deliver

  • Vulnerability assessment by geography and asset class
  • Outage scenario modeling (weather, cyber, N-1)
  • Hardening priority rankings
  • Microgrid and islanding feasibility
  • Resilience metric benchmarking

Ideal For

  • Utilities in high-risk weather zones
  • Post-event recovery planning
  • Resilience grant applications (e.g., FEMA BRIC, LIF)
  • Regulatory resilience reporting requirements

Asset & Inventory Management
