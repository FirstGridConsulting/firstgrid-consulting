Our Services

Comprehensive distribution system engineering — from feasibility studies to long-term strategic planning.

Comprehensive services

Each service delivers technical depth and actionable insights for utility-grade projects.

DER Integration & Hosting Capacity Studies

As distributed energy resources penetrate deeper into distribution networks, utilities need reliable, repeatable methods to evaluate their impact.

  • Hosting capacity assessments by feeder and circuit
  • Detailed interconnection study reports
  • Power flow and short-circuit analysis
  • Voltage regulation & protection coordination review
  • Recommended mitigation strategies
  • GridEdge-based analysis and custom tooling

Ideal For: Utilities evaluating DER interconnection queues, developers planning solar or storage projects, distributors needing rapid capacity screening, regulatory filings requiring technical evidence.

Distribution Feasibility Studies

Before committing to infrastructure upgrades or new interconnections, you need to know what's possible — and what it'll cost.

  • Infrastructure capacity evaluation
  • Upgrade scope and cost estimates
  • Timeline and sequencing recommendations
  • Regulatory compliance review
  • Alternative pathway analysis

Ideal For: New commercial or industrial interconnections, utility capital planning inputs, development project feasibility screening, rate case and regulatory filings.

Distribution Capacity Expansion Planning

Long-term grid capacity planning requires more than trend lines. We combine load forecasting, DER penetration scenarios, and asset condition data.

  • Load forecasting and scenario modeling
  • Grid capacity gap analysis
  • Multi-year expansion roadmap
  • DER impact integration into planning models
  • Priority investment sequencing

Ideal For: Utilities planning for 5–10 year horizons, organizations managing rapid load growth, long-range resource planning cycles, regulatory compliance for capacity standards.

Distribution Resilience Assessment

Extreme weather, aging infrastructure, and cyber threats — the risks to distribution systems are mounting.

  • Vulnerability assessment by geography and asset class
  • Outage scenario modeling (weather, cyber, N-1)
  • Hardening priority rankings
  • Microgrid and islanding feasibility
  • Resilience metric benchmarking

Ideal For: Utilities in high-risk weather zones, post-event recovery planning, resilience grant applications (e.g., FEMA BRIC, LIF), regulatory resilience reporting requirements.

Asset & Inventory Management

Many utilities struggle with incomplete or inaccurate asset data. We help organizations build reliable asset inventories and condition frameworks.

  • Asset inventory audit and gap analysis
  • Condition assessment methodologies
  • Remaining useful life modeling
  • Data quality improvement plans
  • Integration strategy for GIS/AM/FI systems

Ideal For: Utilities conducting asset management reviews, organizations consolidating data from multiple sources, regulatory compliance (e.g., CPUC, NYPA standards), post-merger asset integration.
