About FirstGrid

Built by an engineer. Built for engineers.

20+ years on the distribution grid

FirstGrid Consulting was founded on a simple premise: distribution system engineering deserves better than slide decks and black-box tools.

The founder brings two decades of hands-on experience in distribution system analysis, protection & coordination, substation design, and power flow studies. Throughout that time, a commitment to open-source tooling has shaped a different approach to consulting — one where every analysis is reproducible, every method is transparent, and every deliverable stands up to scrutiny.

This isn't the consulting model you've seen before. There's no bloat, no junior-analyst churn, no generic templates. Just deep domain expertise applied directly to your problem, with the kind of technical rigor that comes from building tools used by engineers worldwide.
